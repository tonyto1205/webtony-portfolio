Kanji Practice App

A Kanji exam practice app that uses Google Sheets to export CSV data, then reads questions and answers from the CSV for efficient spaced review.

Overview

The Kanji Practice App is built around a flexible content pipeline. Question and answer pairs are managed in Google Sheets, exported as CSV, and then loaded in the app to power quizzes and review sessions. This makes it easy to update or expand the question bank without rebuilding the app.
