Bloody Roses

A horror game on Steam where I was responsible for visual effects, character and environment animations, and sound effects to build an intense atmosphere.

Overview

Bloody Roses focuses on slow-burn horror and tension. I created and tuned particle effects, lighting setups, and animation timing to support jump scares and environmental storytelling. I also worked on sound design to reinforce key moments and build atmosphere.
