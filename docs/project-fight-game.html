Fight Game

A combat-focused prototype where I created special effects and one-hit kill animations to emphasize powerful attacks.

Overview

Fight Game explores satisfying hit feedback and combat pacing. I focused on animation timing, impact effects, and camera reactions to make each powerful attack feel distinct and dramatic, even in a prototype environment.
