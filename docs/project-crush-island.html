Crush Island: Choices & Love

A reality TV-style romance game. Stories are authored in Google Sheets, exported as CSV, converted to JSON, and served via Bunny CDN together with Spine characters. Includes Flowise-powered chat rooms plus Firebase, Facebook, and AdMob SDK integration.

Overview

Crush Island is built around a fully data-driven narrative pipeline. Dialogue and branching choices are written in Google Sheets, exported as CSV, and processed into JSON that the game can consume at runtime. Character animations are implemented using Spine, and assets are hosted on Bunny CDN to simplify updates.

Demo & Store Links

The gameplay video showcases branching story choices, Spine character animations, and the integration of live services such as Firebase, Facebook SDK, and AdMob.
